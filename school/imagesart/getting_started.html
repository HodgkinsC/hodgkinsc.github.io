Getting Started with Krita

To install Krita, just go to the download page here: https://krita.org/en/download/

When first opening Krita you will be met with a view similar to this:

On the left should be a tool pallete, for many different tools like the brush tool, line tool, selection tools, etc.
On the right is the colour pallete and the layer selector below it.
