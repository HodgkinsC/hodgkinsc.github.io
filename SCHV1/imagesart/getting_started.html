Getting Started with Krita

To install Krita, just go to the download page here: https://krita.org/en/download/

When first opening Krita you will be met with a view similar to this:

Go ahead and create a new image, with whatever dimensions you want, as long as it's bigger than 1x1 pixels

On the left should be a tool pallete, for many different tools like the brush tool, fill tool, selection tools, etc.
On the right is the colour pallete and the layer selector below it. You may wonder where the eraser is, and that's this button up here:

Unlike many other programs, the eraser in krita isn't actually a tool, but a toggleable setting for other tools. This allows any tool to be the eraser, or any tool that isn't the selection or translation tool.

Over here is the brush pallete, it lets you pick many different types of brush and they'll get applied to the tool when selected. These brushes can be anything, you can even make your own custom brushes if you know what you're doing.
